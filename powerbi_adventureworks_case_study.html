AdventureWorks Sales Dashboard in Power BI

This project is a Power BI report designed for performance monitoring and decision support. It tracks sales growth, profitability, orders, and returns, then allows drill down by product, region, and customer. I also built a pricing simulation so users can test how price changes may affect profit and target performance.

The problem

Leadership needs one report that answers three questions quickly. Are we growing, what is driving results, and what decisions can we test before acting.

What I built

  • Executive page with revenue, profit, orders, and return rate plus category and product drivers
  • Geography page to compare regional performance and concentration of demand
  • Product page with targets and a pricing slider to simulate adjusted profit
  • Customer page showing unique customers, revenue per customer, top customers, and segmentation

Key insights from the report

  • Performance is strong overall, but return patterns still matter because returns can quietly reduce margin
  • Accessories drive the highest order volume, indicating add ons are a major demand engine
  • Shorts show the highest return tendency, which suggests a clear opportunity for fit, sizing, or expectation improvements
  • Demand is concentrated in a few regions, so regional planning is important for marketing and inventory efficiency
  • The pricing simulator helps quantify profit sensitivity and supports product level trade off conversations

Tools used

Power BI, Power Query, DAX, data modelling
