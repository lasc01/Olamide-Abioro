Power BI Sales Dashboard

AdventureWorks performance, returns, customers, and pricing simulator

I built a Power BI report that helps a decision maker track revenue, profit, orders, and returns, then drill into products, regions, and customers. It also includes a pricing simulation to test how price changes may impact profit and targets.

Highlights

  • Executive overview with KPI cards, trend analysis, top categories, and return signals
  • Geography view to compare regional demand and focus
  • Product page with targets and a price adjustment slider showing adjusted profit vs actual profit over time
  • Customer page with revenue per customer, top customers ranking, and segmentation

Tools: Power BI, Power Query, DAX, data modelling

Personalized Music Recommendation

Age and personality based music mood prediction

I built models that connect user attributes, personality traits, and listening patterns to predict music mood and improve recommendation relevance.

Highlights

  • Trained Random Forest, XGBoost, SVM, and KNN models
  • Mapped Big Five traits to listening behavior and mood labels
  • Reached 87 percent accuracy for age prediction and 78 percent accuracy for happiness classification

Tools: Python, Pandas, Scikit learn, Matplotlib

Fake News Detection

ETL plus NLP pipeline with modelling and insights

Built a full pipeline that combines multiple sources, transforms the data, engineers features, trains a classifier, and visualizes patterns between real and fake news.

Highlights

  • Designed ETL across structured and semi structured sources using PostgreSQL and MongoDB
  • Performed NLP preprocessing and feature engineering for sentiment and readability
  • Trained a TF IDF plus Random Forest model with very high accuracy

Tools: Python, NLTK, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, Git
