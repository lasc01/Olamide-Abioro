Hello.

I currently work as a Data Analyst at Imola Schools in Lagos, Nigeria. I have a strong passion for analyzing complex data and turning it into meaningful insights that can drive informed decision
I discovered my passion for IT during the COVID-19 lockdown, which led me to explore various IT courses, including web design, graphic design, and web development. In 2022, I decided to pursue a career in data analytics. Thanks to the coding skills I had already gained from my web development courses, transitioning into data analytics was a smooth process. To further solidify my skills, I took on a one-year internship in 2023, which lasted from January 2023 to January 2024. Following the internship, I secured a position as a Data Analyst at Imola Schools in January 2024, where I am currently employed.
I am proficient in data cleaning, data manipulation, data visualization, analytical thinking, report writing, and more, using up-to-date tools like Python (NumPy, Pandas, Matplotlib), Excel, SQL, and others.
One of my key achievements as a data analyst was creating a form to gather data from the parents of students at my school. Through analyzing this data, I discovered that most parents were either traders or civil servants, which meant they had irregular income streams. Based on these insights, I developed a tailored payment plan where traders could make weekly payments, and civil servants could pay monthly. This application of data analytics significantly improved the school’s cash flow management.
